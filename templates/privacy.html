Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information needed to provide the service:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs (page visits, error reports) to keep the service running reliably.

We do not collect payment card details, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • Authenticate you when you sign in.
  • Diagnose bugs and improve the service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating Spendly.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We apply standard technical safeguards — encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and regular backups — to protect it from unauthorized access or loss.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts: loads typefaces for the interface. Google may log font requests per their own privacy policy.
  • Hosting provider: our infrastructure provider stores data on our behalf under a data processing agreement.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for their own purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also object to processing or request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out through the app or at the contact details provided during registration.
